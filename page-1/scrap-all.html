What is Scrap ? How it will be Useful ?....

Scrap refers to discarded or leftover materials from manufacturing processes or everyday use. It can be useful when recycled or repurposed, reducing waste and conserving resources. Recycling scrap material contributes to environmental sustainability by saving energy and raw materials. Additionally, repurposing scrap for creative projects can promote innovation and resourcefulness.
