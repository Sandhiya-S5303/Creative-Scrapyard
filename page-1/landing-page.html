About Us
Scrap symphony sanctuary is an innovative e-commerce platform designed to revolutionize the utilization of old or discarded items. Serving as a marketplace for individuals to sell their unused or scrap items, our website facilitates a seamless connection between sellers and artists. Artists can purchase these items at affordable rates, transform them into unique, useful, and decorative creations, and subsequently sell their upcycled masterpieces on the platform. This not only provides individuals with an opportunity to earn from their scrap but also empowers artists to showcase their creativity while contributing to sustainable practices
