Why Recycling is important ?....

Recycling is crucial for conserving natural resources and reducing environmental impact. It minimizes the need for raw material extraction, lowers energy consumption in manufacturing, and decreases the volume of waste in landfills. By recycling, we contribute to sustainability, reduce pollution, and promote a circular economy.
